Introduction

Jaejun Park at Hanyang University ERICA specializing in Plasmonics, Surface-Enhanced Raman Spectroscopy (SERS), and Electrocatalysis. Current research focuses on the structural modulation of silica nanocomposites and the development of heterointerface-driven catalysts for efficient water splitting. Integrating experimental chemical synthesis with advanced computational modeling (FDTD and DFT) to design next-generation optical and electrochemical platforms.
